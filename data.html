About Chif's Challenge

Join Chif on his marathon walk through the beautiful, rural country side of Bucks and Berks and raise money for NewLife!

In the spirit of Chif and the challenge there will be no entrance fee. All we ask is you ask your loved ones, work colleagues, bosses and anyone you know to kindly support you on your MARATHON walk! All money raised will go directly to families and children supported by NEWLIFE. Everyone who takes part will receive a 'Chifs Challenge' T-shirt and a medal!

Sponsorship

Thank you for taking on Chifs Challenge.

There is NO entry fee for Chifs Challenge. Instead we ask you to get sponsored. We are passionate about the work Newlife (the charity for disabled children) do and we want to support them as much as possible.

We have created 3 options to make it as easy as possible for you.

  1. Donate on your page (just using the donate button that will be on our page).
  2. You can set up your own separate page linked to Newlife.
  3. Or you can set up your own separate page and link it to our Just giving page through the 'join a team' function.

Here's a link to Just Giving's instructions on how to set up team pages. This will enable all the people who have set up their own pages to join the 'team', which results in them all being listed on your Chif's Challenge page.

The Impact

By taking part in Chifs Challenge you are helping to change local children’s lives. Every penny raised will be spent on vital equipment, such as wheelchairs, specialist beds or car seats which children would otherwise go without. This equipment brings freedom, comfort and safety to those in desperate need. Thank you – together we will make life better.

Meet Chif

We would like to introduce you to Chi Fung Tang. (Chif)

Chif is an enthusiastic and kind hearted person who loves his family and friends, adores Wycombe Wanderers, is passionate about the royal family and has a heart of gold.

He lives in Holmer Green and volunteers at three different workplaces every week, including the Chiltern Food Bank, Work Aid in Chesham, Chiltern Wood recycling and also helps at his local Church for their Pop-in lunches.

Chif has been the official 'Team Helper' on five endurance challenges that our group of friends took on over 8 years. These challenges raised over £120,000 for Newlife, the charity for disabled children.

On Chif's 38th birthday, he was asked what he wanted to achieve this year? He responded with 'I would like to complete a challenge for Newlife'.

And so, "Chif's Challenge" was born. On Saturday June 9th 2018, Chif will be walking 26.2 miles from his front door in Holmer Green to Windsor Castle, to raise money for Newlife.

Chif invites you to take on his marathon too. If you want to challenge yourself to a marathon walk and join Chif in raising even more money, you can sign up from January 2018. There will be a shorter challenge for those who would like to participate on the walk into Windsor.

If you can’t take on your own challenge, Chif would be most grateful if you would show your support by sponsoring him. Every penny raised will go directly towards local families, children and babies supported by Newlife. You will be able to sponsor Chif from January 2018.

Chif has special needs and severe learning difficulties. To us, he is just a very special individual and part of our "gang".

The Chif’s Challenge team want this to be a fun and successful experience for everyone so here are 5 Tops Tips to help you get the most out of Chif's Challenge.

Our 5 Top Tips

1. Socialise

Follow us on social media and keep an eye on what's going on.

2. Train

Get out for a walk.

Chif’s Challenge will take you on a 26-mile marathon from Holmer Green to Windsor. The route follows footpaths and bridleways as far as Bourne End where you will join the Thames Path for a beautiful stroll alongside the river. The final 2.5 miles will take you down The Long Walk with its fabulous views up to Windsor Castle and the finish.

26 miles is a tough challenge and you’ll need to put in some training miles ahead of the big day.

3. Raise

There is NO fee to enter Chif’s Challenge, however, we hope you will ask your very generous family, friends and colleagues to sponsor you via the Chif’s Challenge JustGiving page. Or, if you prefer, you can set up your own JustGiving page and link it to our fundraising page.

All money raised will go directly to Newlife to help disabled and terminally ill children in our local area who need specialist funding. To find out more about Newlife, a charity we have been involved with for over 12 years, click here.

4. Turn up

Chif’s Challenge takes place on Saturday, 9th June. To ensure everyone finishes at a reasonable time, registration will open at 7.30am and close at 8.30am on The Common, Holmer Green, HP15 6TD. You will receive full route information, your Chif’s Challenge t-shirt and everything else you need for the day when you sign on.

5. Enjoy

Join Chif and the team as we raise money for a fantastic cause, be part of a phenomenal challenge and something truly special!

If you have any questions at all, please email chifschallenge@hotmail.co.uk.
