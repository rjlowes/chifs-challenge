About Chif

We would like to introduce you to Chi Fung Tang. (Chif)

Chif is an enthusiastic and kind hearted person who loves his family and friends, adores Wycombe Wanderers, is passionate about the royal family and has a heart of gold.

He lives in Holmer Green and volunteers at three different workplaces every week, including the Chiltern Food Bank, Work Aid in Chesham, Chiltern Wood recycling and also helps at his local Church for their Pop-in lunches.

Chif has been the official 'Team Helper' on five endurance challenges that our group of friends took on over 8 years. These challenges raised over £120,000 for Newlife, the charity for disabled children.

On Chif's 38th birthday, he was asked what he wanted to achieve this year? He responded with 'I would like to complete a challenge for Newlife'.

And so, "Chif's Challenge" was born. On Saturday June 9th 2018, Chif will be walking 26.2 miles from his front door in Holmer Green to Windsor Castle, to raise money for Newlife.

Chif invites you to take on his marathon too. If you want to challenge yourself to a marathon walk and join Chif in raising even more money, you can sign up from January 2018. There will be a shorter challenge for those who would like to participate on the walk into Windsor.
